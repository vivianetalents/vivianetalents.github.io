Senior Android Engineer — Fintech

Recruited a Senior Android Engineer in 2 weeks after the company had been searching unsuccessfully for over 6 months. The hire stabilized market data updates and improved currency wallet reliability for a B2B trading terminal.

About the client

An international product fintech company providing liquidity and technology for Forex, Blockchain, and traditional finance. 400+ people.

Challenge

For 6 months, internal recruiting and multiple agencies couldn’t fill the role. Candidates failed interviews, declined at offer, or didn’t match hard skills and motivation.

Ideal candidate

  • 5+ years in Android development; strong app architecture and database knowledge.
  • Experience integrating mobile apps with backend APIs; excellent Kotlin.
  • Unit and automated testing; CI/CD with tools like Sentry, Grafana, Firebase, Docker.
  • Bonus: chaos engineering, semantic release tooling, Google Associate Android Developer certification.

What we did

  • Analyzed the market and adjusted approach to reach the right candidates.
  • Optimized the process: removed the take‑home test; used case discussions with the team lead.
  • Rewrote the job post to focus on high‑impact candidate triggers.
  • Introduced a first‑call script to quickly filter mismatches.

Results

  • Time‑to‑offer: 2 weeks; offer issued to the second finalist and accepted immediately after the interview (first time in company history).
  • Impact: Optimized market data subscriptions, improving update stability and reducing quote delays.
  • Strengthened internal currency wallets; simplified transaction and conversion logic for a more predictable, transparent UX.
