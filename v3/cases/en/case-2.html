Manual QA with Automation — SaaS (Vilnius)

Hired a Manual QA with automation skills for a Russian-speaking team in Vilnius. The role had been open for 8+ months; we filled it in 1 month. Outcome: fewer critical bugs in production and a faster, more stable release cycle.

About the company

Leading SaaS provider for last‑mile delivery. The team’s only tester was overloaded, slowing quality and releases. Russian language was required to collaborate with a Belarusian team onsite in Vilnius.

Main challenges

  • 8+ months without a successful hire; candidates failed interviews or declined after offers.
  • Insufficient testing expertise inside the team impacted product quality.
  • Needed a hybrid profile: strong manual QA plus practical automation experience across Mobile/Web/SaaS.

What we did

  • Market audit: validated salary/skills feasibility to hire within ~1 month.
  • Optimized process: removed unnecessary steps; added case questions to assess hard skills faster.
  • Rewrote the job post to align with target candidate motivations and value drivers.
  • Adjusted funnel with a structured screening script to filter mismatches early.

Results

  • Time‑to‑offer: 1 month.
  • Impact: Critical bugs in production reduced to a minimum; automation accelerated releases and increased platform stability.
  • Improved collaboration between QA, dev, and analytics raised quality control and reduced bug‑fix costs.
